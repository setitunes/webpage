About Us

We are the Robotics Club of OC

Otahuhu College proudly introduced its first Robotics Club, a trailblazing group of students and teachers eager to dive into the world of robotics. Starting with a passion for technology and a drive to learn, they built and programmed their own robots. Their debut was marked by a successful competition showing, where their inventive designs gained recognition. Since then, the club has expanded its reach, hosting workshops and inspiring others with their enthusiasm for STEM. The Robotics Club has become a cornerstone of innovation at Otahuhu College, sparking curiosity and paving the way for future students.
