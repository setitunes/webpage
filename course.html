The evolution of one of our teams model design throughout their time in the VEX Robotics Competition (VRC).

April (2023)

As first-time participants in the VEX Robotics Competition (VRC), we wanted to design a robot capable of handling specific game tasks effectively. One of the key challenges was developing a mechanism to launch tribals, which led us to explore building a catapult.

August 23'

We were able to come up with ideas on how we were
to enhance our robot's capabilities. One example of these included building an elevator. One that could efficiently lift our robot's weight, providing a strategic advantage during competitions.

Nationals (11th February 2024)

As we advanced in our VEX Robotics Competition (VRC) journey, we decided to incorporate pneumatics into our robot design. The goal was to use pneumatics for our "wings" so this way we can score multiple tribals in a shorter period.
